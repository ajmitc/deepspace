Before you start, right click on the threat deck and shuffle the pack.
Drag & drop to draw threats from there. 

Markers for hull & shields should be at full when you start a new game.

The "Crew Dice" button will show crew dice rolls in the output window.
To play the dice, drag die faces from the "pieces" tab to the board. 

Right click to delete any die face or card.


Any questions, use the Vassal wiki page for this module
